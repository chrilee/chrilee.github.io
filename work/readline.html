Project:

Readline () Magazine

Client | Infusion

Role | Visual Designer

Objective

Readline() is Infusion’s internal magazine that feature articles written by Infusion people for Infusion people. The magazine is design heavy and each article has a different layout that reflects that story.

Solution

These are some of the layouts that I have done for Readline(). Each article looks very different than the next with clean typography. The difficulty with most articles is that they are text heavy which doesn’t leave a lot of room for the graphics. This means that I have to play with the text a lot to make sure that spacing looks proper with no hanging words or lines, and everything has to legible. The graphics have to be carefully thought out to match the article and placed in a way that does not obstruct the text. Due to the professional quality of Readline(), many of the HR and sales people distribute it to new hires and their clients.
