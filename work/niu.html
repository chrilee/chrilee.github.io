Project:

Night It Up!

Website

Client | Night It Up! Night Market

Role | UX / Visual Designer

Description

Night It Up! Night Market is a three night Asian festival event in Markham with over 120,000 attendees.

Objective

They want an easy to use website with a fresh new look that will attract people to the event. The site needs to be easily updatable and managed by a non-developer.

Solution

Due to the fact that the old website used their blog as a homepage, a lot of users miss out on the most important content. This is why for the redesign I made sure that the homepage of the website is designed with relevant content in mind. The homepage features different boxes that the administrator can use to promote important information and also display most updated information by pulling only most recent post from the blog. Large imagery was used to showcase different aspect of the event and to give credibility to the event since it is not a heavily promoted event outside the GTA. Another important consideration for this website is that it should be adaptable to any device and therefore it is design on a responsive grid system that will adjust according to the browser resolution. The website is now live and you can visit it here: www.nightitup.com
