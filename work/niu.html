Project:

Night It Up! Website

Client | Night It Up! Night Market

Role | UX / Visual Designer

Description

Night It Up! Night Market is a three night Asian festival in Markham with over 120,000 attendees.

Objective

Night It Up! wanted an easy to use website with a fresh new look that will attract people to the event. The site needed to be user friendly because it will be managed and updated by a non-developer.

Solution

Due to the fact that the old website used their blog as a homepage, a lot of users missed out on the most important content. For the redesign, I ensured that the homepage of the website was designed with relevant content in mind. The homepage was designed to feature different boxes that the administrator could use to promote important information.. It also had the functionality to display the most updated information by pulling only the most recent post from the blog. Large imagery was used to showcase different aspects of the event and to give credibility to the event since it is not a heavily promoted event outside the GTA. Another important consideration for this website was that it should be adaptable to any device and therefore it was designed on a responsive grid system that will adjust according to the browser resolution.

The website is now live and you can visit it here: www.nightitup.com
